About

My name is Jordan Nichols and I am a Sophmore at the University of New Haven. I am from Bel Air, Maryland and live with my parents and brother. I love playing soccer, eating, and sleeping, but my music is where my heart lies. I got my first guitar at the age of 8 and fell in love. I now play shows around campus and around my home town. I see music as a way to say things that you would never normally say out loud. It allows you to connect with and inspire people.
